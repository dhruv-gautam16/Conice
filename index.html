Who We Are

At Conice, we are a passionate team dedicated to addressing the global challenge of food waste. Committed to sustainability and making a positive impact, we strive to create innovative solutions that transform food waste into valuable resources for a greener and more sustainable planet.

What We Do

At Conice, we have developed a cutting-edge application that revolutionizes the way food waste is managed. Our platform allows individuals, restaurants, grocery stores, and party venues to convert leftover, near-expired, or spoiled food into consumable organic resources. Through features like discounted purchases, surplus swaps, and data analysis, we empower our users to reduce waste, save money, and contribute to building a circular economy. Together, we can make a significant difference in reducing food waste, preserving valuable resources, and creating a more sustainable future for generations to come.
