About

Vedya is an up-and-coming startup based out of Bangalore, India. It was founded by Dhruv Gautam and Gulafsha Ahmed in the year 2022. It was created to bridge the gap between basic healthcare facilities and inaccessible places. Incorporating the most appealing, abundantly available, eco-friendly solar energy into our product to make a robot that could not just check vitals, using sensors, in no time but also provide face-to-face interaction with a qualified doctor. Patients can conveniently and easily get themselves checked up without having to travel long distances for minor, easily treatable ailments. This will utilize the power of robotics in medical applications to save time and resources.
